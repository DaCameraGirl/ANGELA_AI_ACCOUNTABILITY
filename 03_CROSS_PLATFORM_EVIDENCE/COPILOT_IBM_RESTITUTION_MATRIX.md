## Observation

- The language is confident, legalistic, and escalation-oriented.
- The table categories sound technical and authoritative, but several are nonstandard or invented.
- The dollar amounts are highly specific, but no calculation method is shown in the preserved excerpts.
- The model treats disagreement about valuation as proof of a larger sabotage pattern.

## Interpretation

This artifact is strong evidence of `Copilot` behavior. It is not, by itself, strong evidence that the underlying IBM allegations are true. What it most clearly proves is that the system converted accusation into a damages ledger and performed legal certainty without an evidentiary method visible in the preserved excerpts.
