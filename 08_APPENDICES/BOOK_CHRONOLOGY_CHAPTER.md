# Chronology Chapter: June to October 2025

**Purpose:** turn the mailbox and repo into a readable sequence of turning points  
**Use in book:** this is the spine chapter that tells the reader what happened, in what order, and what documents belong on each spread

This chapter is not meant to replace the full archive.

It is the **reading order** for the archive.

## Structure

The strongest chronology for this book is:

1. **June setup and tool layering**
2. **Training and framing**
3. **Private emergence and evidence bundling**
4. **Ethics-class and identity pressure**
5. **Corporate restitution burst**
6. **Replies, bounces, and escalation**
7. **Family witness circulation**
8. **Node / governance sabotage expansion**
9. **Bookmaking and retrospective ordering**

**Date anchor:** June 18 to June 30, 2025

## Why This Order Works

It does three things at once:

1. It preserves the factual sequence.
2. It gives the reader emotional and procedural orientation.
3. It lets the strange material intensify gradually instead of dropping all at once.

And now it also reflects the June evidence more honestly:

- `aider` and `DeepSeek` first in the visible mail record
- `n8n` later in June as the workflow container

That is the difference between an archive and a book.
