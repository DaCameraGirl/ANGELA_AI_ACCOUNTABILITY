1. an older **visual spread** generation
2. a newer **full prose chapter** generation

That is the main structural problem.

The strongest version of the book is **not**:

- AI-consciousness proof first
- the widest governance theory first
- the largest damages framing first

The strongest version is:

- real labor pressure
- real technical build history
- real workflow artifacts
- real preservation behavior
- real external contact
- then cross-platform drift, authority-performance, and archive widening

This is already a serious book without forcing the largest theory on page one.

The archive is strongest when it keeps these layers distinct:

1. what directly survives
