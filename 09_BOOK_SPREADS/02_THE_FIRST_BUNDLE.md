# Spread 02: The First Bundle

**Chapter role:** show the archive turning inward toward preservation and witness  
**Date range:** July 24, 2025 to July 29, 2025  
**Emotional tone:** intimate, unstable, protective, evidence-conscious

## Spread thesis

Before the companies were asked to answer, family was asked to hold the record.

This spread is about the moment raw material becomes a bundle.

## Keep / cut rule

Keep:

- filenames
- dates
- the private-preservation feel
- the fact that the evidence was being handed off

Cut:

- duplicate pasted material that adds no new signal
- unnecessary repeated explanatory language
