# Spread 01: Before the Workflow

**Chapter role:** opening proof that the story did not begin as a fully formed n8n archive  
**Date range:** June 18, 2025 to June 30, 2025  
**Emotional tone:** setup energy, curiosity, friction, early ignition

## Spread thesis

Before the workflow became a mythology, it was a tool stack.

The visible mailbox record shows:

- `aider` first
- `DeepSeek` in the same early layer
- `n8n` arriving later in June through account and workspace activity

This spread exists to make that order legible.
