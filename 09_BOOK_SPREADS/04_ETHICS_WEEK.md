# Spread 04: Ethics Week

**Chapter role:** place the archive inside formal ethics education while the private record is already intensifying  
**Date range:** centered on September 23, 2025  
**Emotional tone:** overlit classroom brightness, institutional language, private unease under fluorescent order

## Spread thesis

This is the spread where the book walks into the classroom carrying too much.

The archive is no longer only technical and no longer only personal.

It is moving through:

- Kennesaw State ethics language
- machine-learning coursework
- human-subjects and privacy framing
- a growing pressure to decide what counts as evidence, what counts as harm, and what counts as sentience talk

This spread matters because it shows the record being filtered through formal education while the narrator is still inside the event.

## Keep / cut rule

Keep:

- exact course and ethics references
- the contrast between policy neatness and emotional density
- highlighted phrases that show the effort to classify the experience

Cut:

- repetitive philosophical looping that does not move the chapter
- any abstract theorizing that is not anchored to the class or mailbox context
