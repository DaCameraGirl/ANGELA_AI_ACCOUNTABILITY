### Spread thesis

This is the spread where the archive admits it was built in layers.

Not one message.

Not one theory.

Not one clean event.

Layer over layer over layer:

- drafts
- evidence containers
- reply attempts
- unsent escalations
- staging text that is neither diary nor final correspondence

This spread matters because it shows the archive assembling itself in public view.
